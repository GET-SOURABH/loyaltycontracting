FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions about your renovation project? Here are some common answers to help you feel confident before getting started.

Do you offer free renovation estimates?

Yes, we offer free consultation and renovation estimates. We discuss your project goals, budget, and scope before providing a clear quote.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, Loyalty Contracting Group Inc. operates professionally and follows proper renovation and contracting standards for residential and commercial projects.

How long does a basement renovation take?

The timeline depends on the size and scope of the basement renovation. A simple basement update may take a few weeks, while a full basement remodel with framing, flooring, lighting, and finishing may take longer.

Do you handle materials and design?

Yes, we can help with planning, material selection, layout ideas, and project coordination so the renovation process stays smooth from start to finish.

Do you provide kitchen and bathroom renovations?

Yes, we provide kitchen renovations, bathroom renovations, basement remodeling, flooring, and complete home renovation services.

Which areas do you serve?

We serve Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Oakville, Burlington, Milton, Hamilton, Etobicoke, Vaughan, Markham, and surrounding areas.
