FAQ

Full House Renovation Questions

A few common questions before starting a complete home renovation.

Do you provide full house renovation services?

Yes. We provide complete home renovation services including interior remodeling, kitchen renovation, bathroom renovation, basement remodeling, flooring, painting, trims, and finishing work.

Can you help with planning and materials?

Yes. We can help you plan the renovation scope, discuss material options, and coordinate the project so the final result feels consistent across your home.

How long does a full house renovation take?

The timeline depends on the size of the home and the amount of work required. After the site visit, we can provide a clearer estimated timeline.

Do you serve the Greater Toronto Area?

Yes. We serve Toronto, Mississauga, Brampton, Oakville, Burlington, Milton, Hamilton, Etobicoke, Vaughan, Markham, and surrounding areas.
